CYBER SECURITY SPECIALIST

A cybersecurity specialist is a professional who protects an organization's computer systems and networks from threats, breaches, and attacks. They assess security measures, implement solutions, and respond to incidents to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data. Cybersecurity specialists often work with various technologies and tools to safeguard sensitive information and collaborate with teams to develop security policies and protocols.

JOB DESCRIPTION:

Cybersecurity specialists analyze, design, and implement security measures to protect systems and networks.

REQUIRED SKILLS:

Network Security: Knowledge of firewalls, VPNs, and intrusion detection systems.
Threat Analysis: Ability to assess and respond to potential threats and vulnerabilities.
Incident Response: Skills in handling security breaches and implementing recovery procedures.
Compliance Standards: Familiarity with regulations such as GDPR, HIPAA, and PCI-DSS.
Communication Skills: Ability to convey complex security concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
